## 2024-07-25 - Icon-Only Buttons Missing Aria-Labels
**Learning:** I've identified a recurring accessibility issue where icon-only buttons are implemented without `aria-label` attributes. This makes them inaccessible to screen reader users, who cannot determine the button's purpose.
**Action:** I will proactively inspect all icon-only buttons for missing `aria-label` attributes in future tasks and add them to ensure the application is accessible.
